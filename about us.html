Robotics & Innovation Club

About Us

The Robotics and Innovation Club of Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (CBIT) was established in 2011 with a mission to explore and advance the field of robotics from its fundamental principles. Our club is dedicated to fostering innovation and creativity among the young minds of CBIT by conducting a variety of workshops, sessions, and hands-on activities.

As a community of enthusiastic learners and creators, our members actively participate in inter-college events, where we've proudly won medals at some of the most prestigious national institutions. Our commitment to excellence in robotics has led to the initiation of our own national-level robotics fest, Robovanza. This event has drawn participation from students across India, making it a landmark event for robotics enthusiasts.

Beyond robotics, our club has successfully undertaken various research and development (R&D) projects. We're also expanding our horizons by engaging in multidisciplinary activities, sparking interest in diverse fields and encouraging holistic development among our members.

Join us as we continue to push the boundaries of innovation and technology, making CBIT a hub for robotics and multidisciplinary exploration.
